    About our Walkway

    Our Hidden Gem

     Hazelwood - Hazelhatch Park Walkway was opened during the first phase of Hazelwood Park development. It connects two beautiful modern housing estates in Celbridge. This walkway is one of the wonders of Celbridge and is too often over looked as a true treasure of Celbridge. This site was established to help celebrate and honor the true beauty of the walkway.

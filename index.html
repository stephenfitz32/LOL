About our Walkway

Our Hidden Gem

Hazelwood - Hazelhatch Park Walkway was opened during the first phase of Hazelwood development. It connects two beautiful modern housing estates in Celbridge. The walkway allows Hazelwood residents easy access to the hazelhatch Park creche, while also allowing Hazelhatch park residents an alternative route to the village. The walkway is designed to allow communities mix. Children use the lane to saftely visit each other. This walkway is one of the wonders of Celbridge and is too often over looked as a true treasure of Celbridge. This site was established to help celebrate and honor the true beauty of the walkway and to reconise its success
